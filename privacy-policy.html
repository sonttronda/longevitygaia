1. Data Controller

Longevity Gaia App
Czech Republic, European Union
Contact: longevitygaia@proton.me
Privacy requests: privacy@longevitygaia.app

2. Data We Collect

Health data (from Health Connect / Garmin / wearables):

  • Steps, heart rate, HRV
  • Sleep duration and quality
  • Other biometric data from connected devices

Profile data (entered manually):

  • Name, age, height, weight, gender
  • Wrist circumference, shoulder width

Activity data (generated by the app):

  • Protocols and completion, NAD+ level
  • Biological age, streak days
  • Meditation history

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Biological age and health score calculation
  • AI-powered personalized recommendations (Gaia assistant)
  • App content personalization
  • Anonymous text translations

4. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))
  • Performance of contract (Art. 6(1)(b))

5. Data Sharing

  • ✅ Supabase (EU servers) — translation storage
  • ✅ AI providers (Groq, Requesty) — anonymized chat and translations
  • ✅ Google Health Connect — reading health data
  • ✅ Google Play Billing — payments (processed by Google, not us)
  • ❌ We do NOT sell data to third parties
  • ❌ No ads, no marketing tracking

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Right of access (Art. 15)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16)
  • Right to erasure / "right to be forgotten" (Art. 17)

  • Right to object (Art. 21)
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Contact: privacy@longevitygaia.app
